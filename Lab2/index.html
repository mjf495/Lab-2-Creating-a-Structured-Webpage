Turkey and Cheese Puff Pastry

Turkey and Cheese Puffs!

I had these before and they are delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 Puff pastry sheet, thawed
  • 2 cups of cooked turkey, chopped
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Nutmeg, dash
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven at 350°. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a Silpat sheet.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry until is doubled in size, and thin
  3. Cut in half to have two sheets.
  4. In a large bowl mix the chopped turkey, 1 egg, ricotta, and mozzarella, dash of nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Divide the turkey and cheese mix in half and place on each puff pastry half, leaving 1 inch space on the three ends. Brush the edge of the pastry with the beaten egg. Bring the puff pastry over the mixture and seal the edges with a fork.
  6. Brush all over the pastry with the beaten egg and place in the warm oven.
  7. Cook for 30-40 minutes or until the top is nice golden.
  8. Remove from oven and let rest for a couple of minutes and serve.
